# Use Cases

Right Suite isn't a one-size-fits-all GTM tool. What you need from it depends entirely on where you are: how much traction you have, what decisions are blocking you, and how fast you need an answer.

The four use cases below map to four different situations. Pick the one that fits now - you can come back to others later.

---

## Who is this for?

### [SaaS Founders Pre-Traction](saas-founders/)
You launched. You have a product and maybe a handful of early users, but you don't have repeatable revenue yet. You're not sure if the problem is your segment, your price, your message, or your channel. Start here to run the full validation sequence in the right order.

### [Agency Founders Repositioning](agency-repositioning/)
Your agency has been running for a while. You know your craft. But prospects don't immediately understand why you're worth more than the cheaper alternative. You need a positioning angle that removes you from commodity comparisons - and copy that communicates it in under 10 seconds.

### [Growth Teams Iterating](growth-teams/)
You have traction, but growth has plateaued. You're testing pricing changes, running copy experiments, and iterating on outreach - but the feedback loop is 4-6 weeks per test. Right Suite compresses that to minutes so you can run more bets per quarter.

### [Vibe Coders & Indie Hackers](indie-hackers/)
You built something with AI or no-code. It works. Now you need to figure out who to sell it to, what to charge, and what to say - without a team, a co-founder, or a marketing budget. The free toolkit gets you moving immediately.
