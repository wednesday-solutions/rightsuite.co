## How It Works

**1. Describe your inputs**
Your offer, price, copy, creative, or outreach sequence - depending on which product you run. A paragraph is enough to start.

**2. Simulation runs**
The engine generates 100+ synthetic buyer interactions. Buyers debate your offer, react to your copy, and decide whether to engage or walk away.

**3. Read your report**
Structured scores, specific objections, buyer language, and a clear next step. Not "here are some things to consider" - here is what to do.

Validate a pricing decision before your next sales call. Test cold email before burning your list. Check whether your page converts before you run paid traffic.
