# Quick Start

Your first validated GTM decision in under 10 minutes.

---

## Who this is for

You built something with AI, no-code, or code. You have a landing page or are about to. You have zero to 10 paying customers. And you're not sure if your price is right, your messaging is working, or you're even talking to the right people.

That uncertainty is the starting point. Here's how to clear it.

---

## Step 1 - Start with audience

Price, copy, and channels all depend on who you're selling to. Get the segment wrong and every other decision compounds the error.

**Run [RightAudience](https://www.rightsuite.co/products/right-audience?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=docs&utm_content=quickstart-step1)** with:
- Your product name and what it does (1-2 sentences)
- 2-4 buyer types you're considering
- The core pain point each one has

You get a purchase intent ranking across segments, willingness to pay by segment, and a clear recommendation on which ICP to target first.

> **No credits yet?** Use [Prompt 01 - ICP Validation]({{ '/prompts/01-icp-validation' | relative_url }}) - paste into Claude or ChatGPT, fill in the brackets, get an answer in 2 minutes.

---

## Step 2 - Validate your price

Once you know who you're selling to, validate what they'd pay - before you put it on your landing page.

**Run [RightPrice](https://www.rightsuite.co/products/right-price?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=docs&utm_content=quickstart-step2)** with:
- Your product name and description
- Your current price
- Your target audience (from Step 1)
- Whether you're SaaS, agency, or other

You get a confidence score, a too-high / optimal / too-low assessment, a suggested price range, and a trial strategy recommendation.

> **Manual version:** [Prompt 03 - Price Sensitivity Test]({{ '/prompts/03-price-sensitivity' | relative_url }})

---

## Step 3 - Test your landing page

Audience validated. Price validated. Now check whether your copy makes the case.

**Run [RightMessaging](https://www.rightsuite.co/products/right-messaging?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=docs&utm_content=quickstart-step3)** with:
- Your headline
- Your subheadline and body copy
- Your CTA
- Who you're targeting

You get a conversion likelihood score, a clarity score, CTA effectiveness, the specific drop-off points, and rewrite suggestions tied to each issue.

> **Manual version:** [Prompt 04 - Copy Scorecard]({{ '/prompts/04-copy-scorecard' | relative_url }})

---

## Step 4 - Run the full sequence

Each product is designed to build on the last. The full sequence:

```
RightAudience -> RightPositioning -> RightPrice -> RightMessaging
     |
RightEngagement -> RightChannel -> RightAd
```

You can run them out of order. But this sequence gives each product the richest input and your results compound.
