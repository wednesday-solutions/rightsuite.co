# Use Cases

Right Suite is built for founders and teams who need to make go-to-market decisions without a research budget, a panel, or weeks of waiting.

---

## SaaS founders pre-traction

**The situation:** You launched. You have a landing page, maybe a handful of signups, but no paying customers yet. You're not sure if the problem is the price, the message, the audience, or the channel.

**How Right Suite helps:**

Run the full 7-product sequence in one afternoon. Start with RightAudience to rank your segments by purchase intent — you'll often discover the segment with the highest intent isn't the one you started with. Then validate your price, test your copy, and check which channels your ICP actually uses.

**What you walk away with:**
- The segment most likely to pay (and why)
- A price point backed by simulated buyer reactions
- Landing page copy that converts strangers, not just people who already know you
- One channel to go deep on first, instead of spreading across all of them

---

## Agency founders repositioning

**The situation:** Your agency has been around long enough that you know what you're good at, but you're struggling to communicate it clearly. Prospects understand what you do but not why to pick you over cheaper alternatives.

**How Right Suite helps:**

RightPositioning shows you how your ideal buyer compares you to competitors — what makes you stand out, what makes you blend in, and the angle that makes you the obvious choice. RightMessaging tests whether your website communicates your edge in 10 seconds.

**What you walk away with:**
- A positioning angle competitors haven't claimed
- A differentiation statement that doesn't sound like everyone else's
- Hero copy that resonates with the buyers you actually want
- Objection language to handle the "why not just use [cheaper alternative]" question

---

## Growth teams iterating on copy and pricing

**The situation:** You're post-traction but your numbers have plateaued. You're iterating on pricing, testing new messaging angles, and running cold outreach — but the feedback loop is slow. A/B tests take weeks. Customers give vague feedback.

**How Right Suite helps:**

Right Suite replaces slow feedback loops with minutes-fast simulation. Test a new pricing tier before you build a new plan. Check if a messaging angle resonates before you run paid traffic to it. Validate a cold email sequence before burning through your list.

**What you walk away with:**
- Confidence scores on pricing changes before you implement them
- Messaging variants ranked by conversion likelihood
- Cold outreach scored for reply intent before you hit send
- Objection language that surfaces what buyers are actually worried about

---

## Vibe coders and indie hackers

**The situation:** You built something with AI, no-code, or code. You have a product that works. Now you need to figure out who to sell it to, what to charge, and what to say — without a co-founder, a marketing team, or a budget.

**How Right Suite helps:**

The free toolkit (prompts + templates + playbook) gets you moving with zero cost. When you're ready for stronger signal, Right Suite runs the simulations for you — 100+ buyer interactions, scored reports, specific next steps — in under 15 minutes.

**What you walk away with:**
- A focused ICP instead of "anyone who needs this"
- A price you can defend to yourself and to prospects
- Copy that explains what you do to someone who's never heard of you
- A channel recommendation based on where your buyers actually are

---

## What Right Suite is not

Right Suite is not a replacement for talking to customers. It's what you do **before** you talk to customers — so you ask better questions, validate faster, and don't burn your list on copy that never had a chance.

It's also not a magic prediction engine. Simulations are models — useful, fast, and often surprisingly accurate, but not a substitute for real-world feedback over time. Use Right Suite to move faster and with more confidence. Use your customers to confirm and refine.
